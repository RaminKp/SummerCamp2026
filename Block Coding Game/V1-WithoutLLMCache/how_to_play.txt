HOW TO PLAY — MISTY MAZE
=========================

GOAL
----
Help Misty the robot navigate through the maze by placing the right
cards in the right order!

YOUR CARDS
----------
  Card 1 (GREEN)  =  Move FORWARD one step
  Card 2 (BLUE)   =  Turn LEFT
  Card 3 (RED)    =  Turn RIGHT

HOW TO PLAY
-----------
1. Listen to Misty's hint — she'll tell you where she needs to go
   and give you a clue about the directions.

2. Choose your cards and place them in the card slots in order,
   left to right (slot 1 = first move, slot 2 = second move, etc.).

3. Press the button to submit your answer.

4. If your sequence is CORRECT — Misty drives to the destination!

5. If your cards are in the WRONG ORDER — rearrange them and try again.

6. If you picked the WRONG CARDS — swap them out and try again.

7. After each leg, remove all the cards from the slots
   and get ready for the next round.

Complete all legs to win the game. Good luck!

EXAMPLE
-------
If Misty needs to go forward, then turn right, then go forward again:
  Slot 1 -> Card 1 (forward)
  Slot 2 -> Card 3 (right)
  Slot 3 -> Card 1 (forward)
  Then press the button!
